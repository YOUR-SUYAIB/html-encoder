About HTML Protector

A free and efficient encryption tool for HTML. Make your code harder to copy and prevent people from stealing your work.

Features:

  • Secure encoding of HTML content
  • Optional pre-loader with customizable duration
  • Domain lock protection
  • Easy to use interface
  • Supports files up to 2MB
